Connect Your Gmail

The tracker reads your Gmail to automatically create and update tasks. You need a Google OAuth Client ID.

How to get your Google OAuth Client ID (one-time setup)

  1. Go to console.cloud.google.com and sign in
  2. Create a new project (or use an existing one)
  3. Click APIs & Services → Library → search "Gmail API" → Enable it
  4. Click APIs & Services → Credentials → + Create Credentials → OAuth client ID
  5. Choose Web application as the type
  6. Under Authorized JavaScript origins, add your GitHub Pages URL:
    https://<your-username>.github.io
  7. Click Create and copy the Client ID
ℹ️ Your emails are processed entirely in your browser — no data is sent to any third-party server.
🚀

All Set!

Your tracker is ready. On first launch it will read your last 30 days of emails and build your task list. After that, it will sync every 10 minutes automatically.
